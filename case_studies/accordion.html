General

Are there any recruiting fees or upfront costs?
No. You only pay the agreed-upon monthly rate.
How does NUMI make money?
The price you pay for NUMI covers everything, including compliance, running a planetary payroll, and the work we do behind the scenes to cultivate Pros as they scale their careers.
What happens if I'm not satisfied with a NUMI Pro?
We allow our you to begin a trial period with up to three experts from our network per role. While we rigorously vet all applicants to ensure skill and intelligence, we understand that not every person will be a perfect fit for every company. For this reason, we allow you to work with multiple experts for each position before you decide on a candidate with whom you are confident and comfortable..
Why do the world's best engineers and designers work with NUMI?
Three reasons: great opportunities, a long-term trajectory, and a network of peers they can grow from.

Geography

Where are your Pros located and are they willing to adjust their schedule?
We gather your schedule requirements on our first call together. The candidates that we present to either already conform to your timezone or are willing to work with you to arrive at a schedule that mutually works.
Where do NUMI Pros work?
NUMI sources the best Pros from around the world. All of them work from home and seamlessly integrate into your team remotely.
May I bring NUMI Pros on-site?
We are remote first. There have only been a few instances where clients have felt it absolutely necessary to bring a NUMI Pro physically on-site. If this truly is a requirement, we can work with you to coordinate relocating an individual or team to your location for a specified period of time for an additional cost.

Intellectual Property

Who owns the rights to the work created by a NUMI Pro?
Your company does! Our contracts ensure the IP produced during the engagement is yours and yours alone.
How do we ensure that our IP is protected?
NUMI enters into agreements with every member of our network. Through these agreements, NUMI obtains exclusive ownership of the work created by the NUMI Pro that works with you. We then provide you with the intellectual property for the work that the NUMI Pro creates for you under our client agreement. This clean, simple, pass-through arrangement has worked well for our clients in the past. Please feel free to reach out to hello@numi.tech if you have additional questions.

Pricing and Payment

How often will I be charged? How are payments and invoices handled?
We will invoice you monthly for the Pro's services.
What methods of payment do you accept?
We will issue payment via a link for you to send payment through our trusted payment provider.
Do you have minimum commitment terms?
For design, the minimum commitment is one month. For engineering, we looking for long-term engagements and try to avoid short term projects.

Testing and Vetting

How are NUMI Pros English?
Each and every NUMI Pro writes and speaks conversational to fluent English. Before we invite prospects to tackle our industry leading, domain-specific tests, we conduct lengthy interviews to ensure they have a high level of proficiency in English and have characters well-suited to working with western technical teams.
How does your vetting process ensure quality candidates that meet our requirements?
We screen for communication skills, personality, domain-specific knowledge, and a high level of professionalism. Less than 3% of applicants pass the screening process. Our process includes a variety of ways to determine skill and proficiency, including portfolio reviews, technical coding challenges, user or market research, business cases, project management, formal assessments, and other advanced assessment criteria. We match all Pros with a NUMI Advocate for each and every interview, and all Pros complete a test project to demonstrate proficiency with a project similar to what they would work on in a typical engagement.
